Student Resources

The Writing Center

The Writing Center and The Speaking Studio at Washington University in St. Louis provide free, one-on-one tutoring to all WashU students for any writing or public speaking project. Its is located in the northwest corner of Olin Library’s first level.

Cornerstone

Cornerstone’s Writing Help Desk provides walk-in help to undergraduate students needing help at any stage of the writing process on virtually any kind of writing, whether for a class or for a job or graduate school application. Open Sun, 8-11pm; Tue & Thu, 7-9pm.
